## Priority Issues

[P1] Eyebrow kicker on every section — remove from inner page headers; one deliberate kicker is voice, reflexive repetition is AI grammar.

[P1] Taupe text contrast fails WCAG AA — #8B7D6B on #FDFAF4 = ~3.3:1, on #F5F0E4 = ~3.1:1. Body text needs 4.5:1. Fix: darken to #6B5E50 for body contexts.

[P1] Pricing cards have no feature copy — users cannot differentiate Refresh Clean vs Full Steam. Pre-launch blocker.

[P2] Services page header dead zone — right column completely empty; either go full-width or pull image up.

[P2] Single font with weak typographic hierarchy — weight 500 display vs weight 300 body is too close in texture.
